Objective 1: Increase Lead Generation
Focus on maximizing the number of qualified leads for DARE's training programs to feed into Pioneer roles.
Objective 2: Optimize Campaign Performance
Ensure all campaigns are cost-efficient, reach the correct audience, and maximize engagement and conversion rates.
Objective 3: High-Quality Trained Candidates
Ensure the training process delivers top-notch talent that meets Pioneer's requirements.
KPI 1: Lead Metrics
Number of leads generated per campaign and platform (META, TikTok, etc.).
KPI 2: Campaign Metrics
Engagement rate, click-through rate (CTR), and cost efficiency of ads.
KPI 3: Training Quality Metrics
Percentage of trainees successfully transitioning into Pioneer roles and time to competence.
Pilot 1: Weekly Reviews
Review campaign data regularly and adjust strategies based on the best performers.
Pilot 2: Daily Adjustments
Use daily reports to monitor lead generation and adjust focus on underperforming areas.
Pilot 3: Feedback Loop
Establish a feedback loop with Pioneer to optimize the training process continuously.
