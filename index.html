Objective 1: Increase Lead Generation
  • Focus on maximizing the number of qualified leads for DARE's training programs to feed into Pioneer roles.
Objective 2: Optimize Campaign Performance
  • Ensure all campaigns are cost-efficient.
  • Reach the correct audience.
  • Maximize engagement and conversion rates.
Objective 3: Provide High-Quality Trained Candidates
  • Ensure the training process delivers top-notch talent that meets Pioneer's requirements.
Objective 4: Monitor Location-Specific Hiring Needs
  • Continuously assess which locations need more talent.
  • Deploy resources to meet these needs effectively.
KPI 1: Lead Generation Metrics
  • Number of leads generated per campaign and platform (META, TikTok, etc.).
  • Conversion rates from leads to enrolled trainees.
  • Cost per lead (CPL) to measure cost efficiency.
KPI 2: Campaign Performance Metrics
  • Engagement rate (likes, shares, comments).
  • Click-through rate (CTR) for ads.
  • Return on ad spend (ROAS).
KPI 3: Training Quality Metrics
  • Percentage of trainees successfully transitioning into Pioneer roles.
  • Time to competence: how quickly trainees reach performance benchmarks.
  • Feedback scores from trainees and Pioneer managers.
KPI 4: Location-Specific Hiring Metrics
  • Number of hires needed per location and language.
  • Lead generation success rate per location.
  • Campaign effectiveness in targeted languages.
Pilot 1: Weekly Performance Reviews
  • Regularly review campaign performance data.
  • Adjust strategies based on the best-performing creatives, texts, and formats.
Pilot 2: Daily Reporting and Adjustments
  • Use daily reports to monitor the status of lead generation and location-specific needs.
  • Adjust campaign focus (e.g., increase spend in locations with low lead flow).
Pilot 3: Feedback Loop with Pioneer
  • Establish a feedback loop with Pioneer.
  • Get insights into the performance of trainees.
  • Adjust training content accordingly.
Pilot 4: Resource Allocation Based on Data
  • Dynamically allocate resources (content creation, ad spend).
  • Target campaigns showing the best lead conversion rates.
