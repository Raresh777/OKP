Objective 1: Increase Lead Generation
Focus on maximizing the number of qualified leads for DARE's training programs to feed into Pioneer roles.
Objective 2: Optimize Campaign Performance
Ensure all campaigns are cost-efficient, reach the correct audience, and maximize engagement and conversion rates.
Objective 3: Provide High-Quality Trained Candidates
Ensure the training process delivers top-notch talent that meets Pioneer's requirements.
Objective 4: Monitor Location-Specific Hiring Needs
Continuously assess which locations need more talent and deploy resources to meet these needs effectively.
KPI 1: Lead Generation Metrics
Number of leads generated per campaign and platform (META, TikTok, etc.).
KPI 2: Campaign Performance Metrics
Engagement rate (likes, shares, comments), click-through rate (CTR) for ads, and Return on Ad Spend (ROAS).
KPI 3: Training Quality Metrics
Percentage of trainees successfully transitioning into Pioneer roles, time to competence, and feedback scores from trainees and Pioneer managers.
KPI 4: Location-Specific Hiring Metrics
Number of hires needed per location and language, lead generation success rate per location, and campaign effectiveness in targeted languages.
Pilot 1: Weekly Performance Reviews
Review campaign performance data regularly and adjust strategies based on the best-performing creatives, texts, and formats.
Pilot 2: Daily Reporting and Adjustments
Use daily reports to monitor lead generation and adjust campaign focus as needed.
Pilot 3: Feedback Loop with Pioneer
Establish a feedback loop with Pioneer to optimize the training process continuously based on performance insights.
Pilot 4: Resource Allocation Based on Data
Dynamically allocate resources (content creation, ad spend) to campaigns showing the best lead conversion rates.
